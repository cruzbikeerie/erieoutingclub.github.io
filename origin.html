Where and when are the club meetings.
The meetings are held the third Tuesday of the month at the The Sight Center of Northwest Pennsylvania 2545 W 26th St, Erie, PA sightcenternwpa.org
Can I try out an event before joining the club?
You can try out an event once before joining the club, but you need to sign a release before doing the event. You can get a release by clicking on join now, then "Click here for membership application". Then download and print out the second page. Sign it and bring it to the event and give it to the event coordinator.
How do I become a member?
Click on Join now, then click on "Click here for membership application" on the main web page. You need to download both the membership application and the release form and send them to the P.O Box which is on the application. You will also need the Acrobat Reader program on your computer which is probably already installed.
Why do I have to pay dues, and what is the money used for?
The Erie Outing Club is a dues-paying group and the majority of our membership fees are donated to groups and organizations that work to create, maintain or improve local trails and wilderness areas.
I joined EOC and I haven't receive a newletter or anything about what going on in the club.
We are a volunteer club, and have a small group of people doing the work. Please remind us by sending an email by going to the contact link on the main web page. From there you can send us a email.You will be then sent an ivitation to join the email list, and if you haven't received a membership card, please inform us that you are still waiting for a membership card.
