When and where are the club meetings

The meetings are held the third Tuesday of the month at the The Sight Center of Northwest Pennsylvania 2545 W 26th St, Erie, PA sightcenternwpa.org

Can I try out an event before joining the club?

You can try out an event once before joining the club, but you need to sign a release before doing the event. You can get a release by clicking on join now, then "Click here for membership application". Then download and print out the second page. Sign it and bring it to the event and give it to the event coordinator.
