About Us

The Erie Outing Club is a group for adults 18 years old and above with the average age between 50-80...

We enjoy social non-competive outdoor activities of all kinds...

Winter Sports

We enjoy Cross-Country skiing, Snow Shoeing, and Hiking in the winter Months...

Backpacking and Hiking

We do day hikes and overnight Backpacking...

Day hike are a great way to get to know other members...

Bicycle Riding

We do everything from flat bike paths to rolling hills out in the country...

The ride are always social, amd most of the time includes eating somewhere on the ride...

Kayaking

We kayak the local streams and rivers in the area...

We also have a group that meets weekly to kayak...

Geezer's

We meet every monday at the Pennsula for a nice easy bike ride...

After the ride we have lunch together to talk about the ride...
